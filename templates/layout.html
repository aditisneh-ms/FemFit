Menstrual Phase

During the Menstrual phase, it's important to take care of your body with gentle, restorative exercises. Light workouts like yoga, walking, and stretching can help reduce cramping and improve circulation.

Recommended workout: Yoga, walking, light stretching.

Diet tips: Focus on iron-rich foods like spinach, beans, and lean meats to replenish the body.

Follicular Phase

The Follicular phase is when your energy levels start rising. This is a great time to engage in moderate-intensity exercises such as cardio or strength training to take advantage of increased stamina.

Recommended workout: Moderate cardio, strength training.

Diet tips: Fuel up with lean protein, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates like quinoa and whole grains.

Ovulation Phase

Ovulation is the peak of your energy and strength. High-intensity workouts and strength training are ideal during this phase, helping you maximize performance and muscle gain.

Recommended workout: High-intensity interval training (HIIT), heavy strength training.

Diet tips: Emphasize protein-rich foods and more complex carbs for sustained energy.

Luteal Phase

As your body prepares for the next cycle, it's important to reduce workout intensity. Focus on moderate exercises like stretching and light strength training, while managing energy levels and mood.

Recommended workout: Low-impact strength training, yoga, stretching.

Diet tips: Consume magnesium-rich foods and avoid sugar to balance energy and mood swings.
